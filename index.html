the zen way..

the simple way to enlightenment

What is zen?

Zen is the path to inner silence, peace and bliss with any rituals or dogma.

Enjoying a beautiful sunrise, listening to the birds and enjoying the beauty of a garden can convey far more about Zen concepts than reading any number of texts explaining the philosophy.

Beyond theories and books

In the path of zen there is no need to have belief in any kind of philosophy, ritual and theories.

One don't need to read books and memorize sutras and teachings.

Enjoying everymoment is zen

When you eat you can eat in awareness or without awareness,

Any thing in life can be done with or without awareness

When lived in awareness, the mind is not chasing after thoughts of past and future,

but is dweling fully in the present moment.

Living in mindful awareness means living in the present moment.

This is zen

Then, every work becomes mediation

When you start living with mindfulness, every act becomes meditation.

Because what else is mediation other than being in the presen moment and not resenting the past and worrying about the future

Zen is about seeing, beauty, bliss and truth in our everyday lives.

Driving to work or washing dishes, every act could easily be meditation.
